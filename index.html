About Us

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Calicut, our restaurant has been serving the finest mussels and seafood dishes to food enthusiasts and families alike. We take pride in sourcing the freshest New Zealand mussels, known for their exceptional quality and taste, while incorporating the rich culinary heritage of Kerala's coastal cuisine.

Our passion for seafood excellence is reflected in every dish we serve. From our signature baked mussels to innovative seafood creations, we combine traditional Kerala cooking techniques with modern culinary artistry to create unforgettable dining experiences. Located in the vibrant city of Calicut, we bring together the best of global seafood with local flavors.
